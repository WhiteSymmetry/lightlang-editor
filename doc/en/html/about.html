About program LightLang Editor 0.7.6

LightLang Editor - the editor of the SL dictionaries. Program was done for creating and editing dictionaried, which are to import to service LightLang ( More ). In LLE everything is thought-out to relieve the process:
  • Auto-search
  • Bookmarks
  • Finding a word upon entering and by button
  • Tags for formatting
  • Zoom in and Zoom out of a text
  • Etc.
